12 June 2020

ATHATH updated:

  • Borgs and AIs now start with their borgHUDs/siliconHUDs on by default.

Jared-Fogle updated:

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Introduces medical wounds, new injuries that can happen to fleshy carbons when they sustain lots of damage on a bodypart. There's quite a lot of change here, but you can read the guide at: https://tgstation13.org/wiki/Guide_to_wounds and an extended changelog is available here: https://hackmd.io/l_FI9b3tSqa_woDadewJXA
  • Introduces scars and temporal scarring! Healing a wound leaves a scar behind that can be seen by examining someone twice rapidly, and if Temporal Scarring is enabled in character prefs, surviving a round with scars will save them to be granted at roundstart another round! Let your body tell stories!
  • Bleeding is now fully bodypart-focused, and 95% of bleeding comes from cut wounds from sharp weapons. Gauze is applied on a limb-by-limb basis, and helps staunch bloodflow rather than totally stop it. Notably, you no longer bleed just from having 40+ brute damage on a limb.
  • Organic bodyparts are no longer disabled at maximum damage, but are easier to cause wounds to
  • O2 medkits in emergency lockers have been replaced with new emergency medkits with basic tools for diagnosing and treating wounds and basic damage
  • Herapin now rapidly increases bleeding on all open cuts, rather than causing bleeding by itself. The more cuts on the target, the more it will affect them.
  • Neckgrab table slams now hit the targeted limb rather than just the head, with a large chance to dislocate or break a bone
  • Sharp weapons and burning weapons can now shred zones on jumpsuits, disabling protection on that limb. Damaged clothes can be repaired with cloth.
  • Slaughter demons now deal less raw damage, but gain the ability to cause cut wounds, which becomes more powerful with each attack on a humanoid but resets when bloodcrawling.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Xenobiology's Winter Coats now have a new set of pockets that can hold all the same equipment as a lab coat.
  • All Xenobiology secure containment areas there were missing them, now have Connector/Injector pairs for injecting gasses directly into secure containment.
  • Pubby's Xenobiology lab now has three slime spawns.
  • Pubby's Xenobiology lab has been expanded with addtional containment pens.
  • Meta's Xenobiology freezer no longer shares a pipe with Xenobio's air distribution pipes. Scientists should no longer freeze to death and space suits with internals are no longer necessary.
  • Delta's Xenobiology lab now requires Xenobiology access to enter.
  • Delta's Xenobiology lab has been expanded with additional containment pens.
  • Pubby's Toxins Mass Driver room now has an intercom.

  • Prototype Hardsuits have been moved to the RD's Office on from Toxins on Meta and Pubby.
  • All Genetics Labs across all station now come fully equipped with Body Bags, Space Cleaner and Mutadone.
